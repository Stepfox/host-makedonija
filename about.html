About Host Macedonia

Welcome to Host Macedonia. We are an independent review and comparison platform dedicated to evaluating web hosting providers and server performance specifically for users, businesses, and developers in North Macedonia.

Our Mission

Finding the right web hosting can be a daunting task with thousands of options available worldwide. Our mission is to simplify this decision by providing transparent, data-driven comparisons. We test popular global hosting platforms based on speed, latency, reliability, security, customer support, and value for money, allowing you to choose the host that fits your specific needs.

Our Testing Methodology

We believe in concrete data, not marketing slogans. Our testing methodology involves:

  • Skopje-Based Latency Tests: We continuously measure response times (ping rates) and TTFB (Time to First Byte) from servers situated in Skopje, Macedonia, to ensure accurate local loading speeds.
  • Uptime Monitoring: We track monthly and annual uptime percentages of active test websites to verify that providers meet their 99.9% uptime guarantees.
  • Support Evaluation: We contact customer support teams at various times of the day to test response times and engineering helpfulness.
  • Pricing and Value Analysis: We inspect renewal fees, storage limits, bandwidth limits, and included features (like free domains, backups, and SSLs) to evaluate overall budget value.
